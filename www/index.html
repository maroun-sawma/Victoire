ABOUT US
 

About Victoire

Having been in the insurance business since 1954, Ibrahim SULTANEM established Victoire Insurance Company s.a.l. in 1987 with great dedication and commitment.

The Company was registered at the Commercial Register in Beirut under No. 52907.

Since then, Victoire has been gradually growing and what began as a small family business has evolved into a well reputed joint-stock company with over of ten groups of shareholders.

Victoire, with the 59 years of experience of its management in the insurance field, and with a high standard of ethics and professionalism, continues to serve its clients all across Lebanon.

  • Victoire is backed-up by AAA rated first class reinsurers.
  • Victoire has a capital amounts US$10,000,000 fully paid.
  • Victoire has a very high solvency ratio.
  • Victoire is also compliant with all required guarantees at the Ministry of Economy.
  • Victoire’s medical insurance portfolio is managed by GlobeMed (previously known as MedNet Liban) since 1993.
  • Victoire is a member of ACAL (Association des Compagnies d’Assurances au Liban).

Victoire Insurance operates in all major cities all across Lebanon and its sales power is expanding in order to be able to cover all its clients or future prospects needs.

Victoire faced up the challenge and secured uninterrupted services to its clients over the years. Moreover, Victoire’s stability allowed us to securely overcome the Worldwide Economic Crisis.


Mission

Our mission is to serve our clients and our country by sharing our long term experience in insurance and prevention.


Vision

Victoire Team role is to spread awareness & Prevention through our insurance experience


We Prevent & Educate,
You Insure.
